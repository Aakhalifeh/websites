Kareem Abdul-Jabbar is an American retired professional basketball player who spent 20 seasons with the Milwaukee Bucks and Los Angeles Lakers in the National Basketball Association (NBA). Abdul-Jabbar won the NBA Most Valuable Player award a remarkable six times while playing center. (MVP). He was selected to the NBA All-Star team an unprecedented 19 times (tied for the most ever), 15 times (all-NBA team), and 11 times (all-defensive team). As a player, he was a part of six NBA title teams, and as an assistant coach, he was a part of two more. He was twice selected as the NBA Finals MVP.
