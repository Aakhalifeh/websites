American professional basketball player LeBron Raymone James Sr. plays for the Los Angeles Lakers in the National Basketball Association. (NBA). He is known as "King James" and is frequently compared to Michael Jordan in discussions about the greatest basketball player of all time. James is regarded as one of the all-time greats in the sport. James rates fourth in career assists and is the all-time top scorer in the NBA. He has participated in 10 NBA Finals and earned four NBA championships—two with the Miami Heat, one with the Los Angeles Lakers, and one with the Cleveland Cavaliers. He has two Olympic gold medals, four MVP trophies, and four Finals MVP awards.

LeBron James was the #1 player in the nation as a high schooler playing for St Vincent-St Mary High School in Akron, Ohio, and was drafted #1 overall in the 2003 NBA Draft by the Cleveland Cavaliers, he is apart of arguably the greatest NBA draft class(Other than 1984) of all-time.

13 years later, he took the Cavaliers, single-handedly to the NBA Championship for the first time in franchise history off the back of the greatest ever NBA Finals Comeback of all-time.
